GAME RULES

SINGLE PLAYER: YOU WILL CHOOSE FIRST, THEN IT WILL SHOW THE RESULT AGAINST THE COMPUTER FOR THAT ROUND. CLICK NEXT TO CONTINUE ROUNDS. IF THERE ARE NO MORE ROUNDS. IT WILL SAY WHETHER YOU WON OR LOST!

MULTIPLAYER: PLAYER ONE WILL CHOOSE FIRST, THEN PLAYER TWO. IT WILL SHOW THE RESULTS FOR THAT ROUND ONCE PLAYER TWO DECIDES. CLICK NEXT TO CONTINUE ROUNDS. IF THERE ARE NO MORE ROUNDS. IT WILL SAY WHETHER PLAYER ONE OR PLAYER TWO WON!

IF IT'S A TIE, THE GAME WON'T BE COUNTED! IT'S BEST OF (NUMBER) WINS, NOT ROUNDS.

WHAT WINS

  • SCISSORS CUTS PAPER
  • SCISSORS DECAPITATES LIZARD
  • ROCK CRUSHES LIZARD
  • ROCK CRUSHES SCISSORS
  • PAPER COVERS ROCK
  • PAPER DISPROVES SPOCK

  • LIZARD POISONS SPOCK
  • LIZARD EATS PAPER
  • SPOCK SMASHES SCISSORS
  • SPOCK VAPORIZES ROCK
